Apply now to be a part of The Agency team for the Summer/Fall.





The Agency is currently recruiting the best and brightest students at the University of Florida for its 2017-2018 team.

 
 
Applications will open on Monday, March 27th and close on Sunday, April 2 at midnight. Interviews will begin immediately for those moving onto the next round, so do not wait to submit your application. Students from any major or grade-level are eligible to apply, including graduate students. Currently, our greatest need is for students graduating in Spring 2019 or later.  
 
The Agency was established to offer an immersive learning experience to public relations and advertising students and to provide them with the competitive advantage necessary to excel in the job market. We are led by professionals, staffed by students and inspired by faculty.  
 
We require a 10-15 hour time commitment from all of our team members. Working in The Agency requires a strong work ethic, and you get out what you put in.  
 
The Agency's positions fall into five pillars: Catalysts (accounts), Connectors (media), Creators (creative), Strategists (research) and Administrators (admin). Position descriptions can be found inside the respective pillar's application. Please only submit applications to a maximum of two different pillars.

If you have any questions, please contact: theagencyrecruitment@gmail.com
